# Governance

SeeBot uses transparent, pull-request-based governance. Maintainers approve code and documentation changes. Cohort decisions and ambiguous audit outcomes require a recorded reviewer and evidence. Material protocol changes require two approvals after the pilot freeze.

Conflicts of interest, including maintainership of an audited project, must be declared in the relevant review record. Such reviewers may provide factual corrections but should not be the sole adjudicator.
